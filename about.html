    • About this website



    Motorex is a combination of two words, "motor" and "rex."
    "Motor" is a word commonly used to describe vehicles powered
    by an engine, while "rex" is a Latin word meaning "king."
    Therefore, Motorex can be interpreted as "King of Motors"
    or "King of Vehicles". We strive to provide you with the
    most up-to-date information about cars and motorbikes while
    ensuring that it is reliable and from a trusted source. This
    website has been published to give users a free and refreshing
    experience without any ads. As we are still a relatively new
    company, our services will get even better in the future.
    So, stay tuned and please keep visiting our website.
    Thank you
